Now

It’s been 2 years since I moved here. Each time I visit home, I experience a mix of joy, nostalgia and sadness. I’m glad to see the wonderful people that have been a part of my life for most of it, but on the other hand, I am reminded of the reasons why leaving was a good idea.

I’ve moved at the height of the pandemic, just when the country went into hard lockdown. It was months until things started returning to normal, and I’ve been mostly working from home for three years.

Even though I am still looking for my place here, I feel settled. I’ve met some wonderful supportive people and the city has a lot to offer.

Currently I’m working on:

  • My health and well-being. My exercise goals are more humble now, but I hope I can maintain them for longer.
  • Posting more online.
  • Music production. I have recently acquired an Ableton Push 2, and am having a great time with it.
  • Reading more. I’m taking a more holistic approach, with trying to read more books in parallel. Previously I found myself binging books in a series and just burning out. Now I want to take things easy and enjoy it more.

This page contains what I’m focusing on, inspired by Derek Sivers. Updated 2022-11-09.
