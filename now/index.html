Now

The pandemic has made the world a scarier place. As the measures transitioned from extreme to completely lax, so too did I reemerge from my room. The first sights of “outside” felt quaint and strangely nostalgic - as if I have returned from a long and taxing journey, and everything was just slightly, but unsettlingly different.

Currently I’m working on:

  • Moving to Berlin for a new career opportunity.
  • Exercising three times a week and being mindful of my diet.
    • I’m doing the r/bodyweight Recommended Routine. I find it sophisticated and clear enough to suit most peoples needs, and it certainly works for me.
    • I log my food and calorie intake.
  • Meditating every day.
  • Composing music.
  • Reading every day.
    • My goal is to read at least 24 books until the end of the year. I’m slightly behind schedule due to taking a break, so I’m trying to make up for that.
  • Getting this site off the ground.
