Now

It’s been 2 years since I moved to Berlin. The distance, new environment and circumstance changed me. Each time I visit home, I am washed by a torrent of emotions. Joy, nostalgia and sadness in the same time. Happy and nostalgic because I get to see the wonderful people that have been a part of my life for most of it. Sad, because I am reminded of the reasons why leaving was a good idea.

Here, sometimes I feel alone. I’ve moved at the height of the COVID pandemic, just when Germany went into hard lockdown. It was months until things started returning to normal, and I’m still mostly working from home.

Even though I am still looking for my place here, I have settled in and consider this home.

Currently I’m working on:

  • My health and well-being. My exercise goals are more humble now, but I hope I can maintain them for longer.
  • Posting more online.
  • Music production. I have recently acquired an Ableton Push 2, and am having a great time with it.
  • Reading more. I’m taking a more holistic approach, with trying to read more books in parallel. Previously I found myself binging books in a series and just burning out. Now I want to take things easy and enjoy it more.

This page contains what I’m focusing on, inspired by Derek Sivers. Updated 2022-11-07.
